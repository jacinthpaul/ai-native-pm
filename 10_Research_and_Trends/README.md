# 10 — Research and Trends

Curated pointers to external research, papers, reports, and emerging signals — with PM-focused takeaways.

## What This Section Covers

The AI landscape moves fast. This section doesn't try to summarise everything — it curates what actually matters for practicing PMs and explains why.

## Editorial Policy

- Every entry includes a "PM takeaway" — the "so what" for a practicing PM
- Links only for external content — no reproducing copyrighted material
- All entries include a date; stale links are pruned quarterly
- We are not affiliated with any of the sources listed here

## How to Contribute to This Section

Suitable contributions for `10_Research_and_Trends/`:
- New reading lists for specific topics (AI in fintech, AI evaluation methods, etc.)
- Updates to existing lists (add new resources, mark stale links)
- New trend watch articles (quarterly snapshots of emerging signals)

Every reading list entry must include: Title, Source/Author, Year, PM Takeaway, and Link.

Please follow the format standard in [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md).
