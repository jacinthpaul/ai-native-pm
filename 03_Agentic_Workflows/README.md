# 03 — Agentic Workflows

Designing, managing, and evaluating multi-step AI agent systems — from the PM's chair.

## What This Section Covers

As AI systems move from answering questions to taking actions, the PM's role expands to defining the scope of autonomy, the guardrails, and the human oversight model. This section covers what PMs need to know about agentic AI — not as an engineering concern, but as a product design responsibility.

## Suggested Reading Order

1. [Agentic AI: A Primer for Product Managers](./agentic-pm-primer.md)
2. [Designing Human-in-the-Loop Checkpoints](./designing-human-in-the-loop-checkpoints.md)

## Topics Covered

- What agents are and how they work (for PMs)
- The agent design decisions that belong to the PM
- Human-in-the-loop checkpoint design
- Failure modes unique to agentic systems
- Orchestration patterns and subagent roles
