# 07 — Metrics and KPIs

Frameworks and specific metrics for measuring AI products and AI-assisted PM work.

## What This Section Covers

Traditional product metrics — retention, engagement, conversion — are necessary but not sufficient for AI features. AI products introduce new failure modes, new quality dimensions, and new operational costs that require their own measurement layer. This section covers the full stack of metrics an AI PM needs to define and own.

## Suggested Reading Order

1. [Measuring AI Feature Performance: A PM's Framework](./measuring-ai-feature-performance.md)
2. Use the [AI Metrics Scorecard Template](./template-ai-metrics-scorecard.md) to track your own AI features.

## Topics Covered

- User outcome metrics for AI features
- AI quality metrics (acceptance rate, edit rate, fallback rate)
- Operational metrics (latency, cost, error rate)
- Business impact measurement
- Evaluation design for AI features
