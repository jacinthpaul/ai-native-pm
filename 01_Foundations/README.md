# 01 — Foundations

Ground-level concepts every AI PM needs before diving into practice.

## What This Section Covers

This section builds the foundational literacy that separates an AI-native PM from a PM who occasionally uses AI tools. It covers mental models, vocabulary, the nature of LLMs, and the evolving expectations of the PM role in AI-driven teams.

**If you're new to AI PM, start here.**

## Suggested Reading Order

1. [What Is an AI-Native Product Manager?](./what-is-an-ai-native-pm.md)
2. [GenAI Mental Models for Product Managers](./genai-mental-models-for-pms.md)

## Topics Covered

- Defining "AI-native" in the context of product management
- Core competencies of an AI PM
- Mental models for understanding LLM behaviour
- The shift from deterministic to probabilistic thinking

Suitable contributions for `01_Foundations/`:
