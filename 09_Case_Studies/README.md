# 09 — Case Studies

Real-world (and realistically anonymised) examples of AI PM in practice.

## What This Section Covers

Theory is only useful when you can see how it plays out in the real world. This section collects practitioner case studies — honest accounts of what was built, how it went, what worked, and what didn't.

## Anonymisation Policy

Companies and individuals can be anonymised. The situation must be real. If you're submitting a case study anonymised for confidentiality, note this in the `Anonymized: Yes` field of the template.

## What Makes a Good Case Study

- Clear before/after: what was the problem, what changed?
- Honest about failures: what didn't work and why?
- Quantified where possible: numbers beat adjectives
- PM-focused: the lesson should be actionable for another PM
