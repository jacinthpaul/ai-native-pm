# 02 — AI-Native PMO

AI-native approaches to product management rituals, documentation, and team operations.

## What This Section Covers

This section covers how the practice of product management itself changes in an AI-native environment — not just using AI tools to go faster, but rethinking how discovery, documentation, and decision-making work when AI is in the loop.

## Suggested Reading Order

1. [AI-Assisted Product Discovery](./ai-assisted-product-discovery.md)
2. [Writing PRDs in the Age of AI](./ai-native-prd-writing-guide.md)

## Topics Covered

- AI-assisted discovery and research
- PRD writing with AI support
- Roadmapping and prioritisation in an AI-native team
- Stakeholder communication and async updates
- Decision-making frameworks adapted for AI products

Suitable contributions for `02_AI_Native_PMO/`:
- Guides on how specific PM rituals change with AI (planning, retrospectives, reviews)
- Prompt patterns that work well for PM workflows
- Before/after comparisons of PM processes with and without AI
