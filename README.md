# ai-native-pm

> An open-source knowledge base for AI-native Product Managers.

Built and maintained by [Jacinth Paul](https://github.com/jacinthpaul) / [PSHQ](https://pshq.com) — a learning community for PMs working with AI and emerging tech.

---

## What Is This?

`ai-native-pm` is a practitioner-first knowledge base covering the full spectrum of what it means to work as a product manager in an AI-native environment — from foundational literacy to agentic workflow design, metrics, templates, and real-world case studies.

This is not a theoretical resource. Every piece of content is written to be picked up and applied immediately.

**Who this is for:**
- PMs transitioning into AI-native roles
- Practicing PMs building AI features or managing AI products
- Educators and community builders in the PM/AI space
- Anyone who wants to contribute to the practice of AI PM
