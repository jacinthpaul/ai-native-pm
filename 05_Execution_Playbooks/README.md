# 05 — Execution Playbooks

Step-by-step operational guides for recurring AI PM activities.

## What This Section Covers

Playbooks are not theory — they're checklists you pick up and run. Every playbook in this section has a clear trigger (when to use it), a sequence of steps, expected outputs, and common gotchas.

## Suggested Reading Order

1. [Playbook: Launching an AI Feature](./playbook-ai-feature-launch.md)
2. [Playbook: Running Sprint Ceremonies with AI Assist](./playbook-running-ai-sprint-ceremonies.md)

## Topics Covered

- AI feature launch and staged rollout
- Sprint ceremonies adapted for AI-assisted teams
- Model evaluation and selection
- Incident response for AI feature failures
- Onboarding new PMs onto AI-native workflows

## How to Contribute to This Section

Suitable contributions for `05_Execution_Playbooks/`:
- Playbooks for AI PM activities not yet covered
- Refinements to existing playbooks based on real experience
- Gotcha sections based on what went wrong when you ran these

Every playbook must include: a **Trigger** (when to use it), numbered **Steps**, **Outputs** (what you have at the end), and **Common Gotchas**.

Please follow the format standard in [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md).
